About

I'm research assistant at ISR-IST, my main research interests are in Computer Vision, Robotics, Optimization, and Machine Learning.

Currently, I'm working on the Feedbot project. This project is a collaboration between ISR-IST and Carnegie Mellon University for the development of an autonomous robotic feeding assistant.

I did my bachelor and master studies in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico.

During my master's thesis, I had the pleasure of being advised by Manuel Marques and João Paulo Costeira. My master's thesis was in the scope of the Feedbot project and it was focused on the development of Computer Vision techniques to detect the head-pose of the user and to have feedback on the acquisition of the food from the plate. During this time, I had the pleasure of working 2 months with Travers Rhodes at CMU invited by Manuela Veloso.
